Introduction

Terraria is a sandbox adventure game developed by the company Re-Logic and was released for Microsoft Windows on the 16th of May in 2011. Dubbed the “2D Minecraft”, it’s a game of survival and creative building. You get to customise and explore a vast land (picking from a small, medium or large world) and fight off evil (the Corruption or Crimson biomes) in order to reach the ultimate end goal, defeating the Moon Lord (the end game boss).
