Fun Facts

  • Re-Logic were in the works of a new game, a spin-off or “sequel” of Terraria called “Terraria: Otherworld” but ultimately cancelled it due to wanting to branch off from Terraria and pursue brand new projects.

  • Journey’s End is Terraria’s last and final update, with 9 years after launch, the game is finally complete. It took Re-Logic 4 years of consideration and development to release it, which in turn made it one of Terraria’s biggest and most anticipated events since its launch in 2011.

  • Re-Logic and Mojang (Minecraft’s developer and publisher) have quite an interesting friendship, as sometimes when you launch Minecraft, one of the few splash texts on the title menu says, “Also try Terraria!”. The same happens vice versa, when you launch Terraria, one of the few texts it can say next to the title in the GUI is “Also try Minecraft!”.

  • Additionally, during Halloween, one of the costumes you can get in Goodie Bag drops is the Creeper costume!
